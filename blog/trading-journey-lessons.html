My Trading Journey: Lessons from the Markets

May 15, 2026 · 7 min read

Trading has been one of the most rewarding and humbling experiences of my life. What started as curiosity about the markets has evolved into a disciplined practice combining technical analysis with algorithmic strategies.

The Beginning

Like many retail traders, I started with small capital and big dreams. The early days were a rollercoaster — euphoric wins followed by crushing losses. I learned the hard way that emotions are a trader's worst enemy.

Key Lessons

  • Risk Management First — Never risk more than you can afford to lose. Position sizing is more important than entry timing.
  • Keep a Journal — Writing down every trade helped me identify patterns in my decision-making.
  • Automate What You Can — Removing emotion from execution was a game-changer for my consistency.
  • The Market Is Always Right — Fighting the trend is a losing battle. Learn to flow with it.

Building Algorithms

Transitioning from manual to algorithmic trading was a natural progression. I started with simple moving average crossovers in Python, then gradually built more sophisticated systems incorporating multiple indicators and risk management rules.

The journey continues, and every day brings new lessons. The key is to keep learning, stay disciplined, and never stop improving.
